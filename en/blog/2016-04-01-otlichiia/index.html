Differences

April 01, 2016

I talked with her. The voice is quiet, insinuating, almost inaudible… But when she sings, the voice is so different, as from a different person: loud, rather high, with some crying, sometimes breaking. How much, after all, the common singer voice sometimes differs from the singing one.
