Triple duplication or paranoia

September 04, 2014

I was looking for a method for automatic backup through CWM and found Online Nandroid Backup * ROOT. I read about the danger of live file system backups but my device is officially supported and I’ve tested several live backups restoring. No problems.

Finally, I have recurring backups through Titanium Backup ★ root, Online Nandroid Backup * ROOT, and standard android. This is paranoia, isn’t it?
